Which Collection Is Right for You

Choosing the right coverage starts with thinking about your wedding day timeline and what moments matter most to you. Six hours of coverage works beautifully for intimate weddings and elopements, giving us time to capture your ceremony, family portraits, and couple portraits with artful intention. If you're planning a full wedding day with getting ready coverage, ceremony, cocktail hour, reception, and dancing, 8 to 10 hours allows us to document your entire celebration from start to finish. A second photographer means we can be in two places at once, capturing both perspectives simultaneously and ensuring nothing is missed. Let's discuss your vision, walk through your timeline together, and create a photography plan that fits your day perfectly.
